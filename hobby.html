My Hobbies

When I’m not busy with courses, I love spending time on hobbies that make me happy and keep me entertained. Some let me be creative, others help me unwind, and a few are just for pure fun. They’re little things I look forward to every day!

Crocheting

I want to get better at crocheting so I can make my own stuffed animals and tiny keychains to collect (or give as gifts).

Music

Music is always part of my day. I like discovering new artists, curating playlists, or getting lost in my favorite songs. It inspires me and keeps me focused while studying.

Journaling

Sometimes I’ll pick up my journal when I need it most, especially after a tough day. It’s my way of clearing my head.

Photography

Most of my camera roll is full of flowers, sunsets, and cats — and I wouldn’t have it any other way.

Gaming

I spend a lot of time playing Minecraft, especially building cool things. I also love playing with friends.
